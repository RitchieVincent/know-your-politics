How this will work!

You will be presented with a series of 10 random political policies. Each policy is a statement given by one of the 4 main UK political parties; Labour, Conservative, Liberal Democrat and UKIP.

Along with each policy, a timer will run across the top of the text area, indicating that you have 10 seconds to answer, before it moves on to the next policy.

Each policy will have a tick and a cross button, indicating agree or disagree respectively. If you agree with that policy, regardless of the party you have selected, you should click the agree button. If you disagree with the policy you read, you should click the disagree button. Simple!

After the 10 policies have been displayed, you will be presented with a results page. In this results page, it will show how many times you agreed with your chosen party. Remember, the policies are random, so you may get asked 0 policies relating to your chosen party. In this case it will display 0%.

You will also be shown a graph, indicating which party you have most agreed with, and some handy links for you to further your reading about the party you seem to most agree with.
