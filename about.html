Who?

Our team is made up of 3 students: Ritchie Vincent, Lisa Waddington and Azhar Kiani.

We are all final year students at The University of Huddersfield.

We created the Know Your Politics website over the course of 4 months.

Why?

47% of young people (Aged 11-18) in Britain admit to not knowing much or anything at all about The Conservative Party, the current party in power. This number rises with The Liberal Democrats and The United Kingdom Independence Party.

This shows a clear disconnect with young people in Britain and politics. This website will allow not just young people, but people of all ages to learn more about politics.

58% of young people asked said they know at least a little about The Labour Party. This website will also put that knowledge to the test.

The aim of this website is to put people's knowledge of the party they think they support to the test. It will challenge your existing knowledge and ask the question, do you really Know Your Politics?
